ABOUT THIS APP
What's That HAZMAT uses AI to instantly identify and explain DOT HAZMAT placards — the diamond-shaped warning signs on trucks and rail cars. Point your camera at any placard to get plain-English information about the hazard, UN number, danger level, and emergency guidance.
HOW TO USE
1
Take a photo of any HAZMAT placard using your camera or upload an existing photo.
2
Tap Analyze Placard to send the image to our AI for identification.
3
Review the results — hazard class, UN number, danger level, and emergency actions.
4
Browse the tabs for news, supplies needed, and premium features.
